Our Story

My journey with Kabale Online started when I was reporting for my first year at university. I had very little money, but I needed to furnish my small room with the essentials: a table for studying, a chair, and a small woofer for some entertainment. I knew I wanted to buy them second-hand to save money, but finding sellers was incredibly difficult. I walked around town, asked friends, and checked notice boards, but it was a frustrating and time-consuming process. I thought to myself, "There has to be a better way."

I couldn't find the items I needed, but I did find an idea. I decided to develop this website, Kabale Online, to solve the very problem I was facing. My goal was to create a simple, free, and centralized place where students, families, and anyone in Kabale and the wider Kigezi region could easily connect to buy and sell second-hand goods. I wanted to help other students who were in the same situation I was in, and to empower local residents to make some extra money from items they no longer needed. This platform is built for our community, by one of its own.

The Role of Kabale Online

Our role is to be the platform that connects buyers and sellers. Please be aware: we do not own any of the products listed on this site. All transactions are handled directly between the buyer and the seller.
