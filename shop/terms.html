1. Buyer Protection Policy

Our goal is to prevent fraud and scams. We have implemented the following measures to protect you:

1.1 Verified Sellers

Sellers marked with a Verified badge have undergone an identity verification process with us. While this significantly reduces risk, we still advise caution with all transactions.

1.2 Safe Transaction Guidelines

To protect yourself from theft or scams, we strongly recommend following these steps:

  • Meet in Public: Always arrange to meet the seller in a safe, public place or any open place .
  • Inspect Before You Pay: Thoroughly inspect the item to ensure it matches the description and is in the expected condition before handing over any money.
  • No Prepayments: Do not pay for an item before you have received and inspected it. Be wary of sellers who demand advance payments or deposits via mobile money or bank transfer.
  • Bring a Friend: If possible, take a friend with you to the meeting.

1.3 Dispute Resolution

If an item is significantly not as described or if you suspect fraudulent activity:

  1. First, attempt to resolve the issue directly with the seller.
  2. If you cannot reach a resolution, contact us within 48 hours of the transaction through our official support channel.
  3. Provide all evidence, including screenshots of your chat, photos of the item, and the seller's profile details.
  4. NB : Please remember its your responsibility to ensure that your safety is at maximum.

2. Prohibited Items and Conduct

To protect all users, the sale of the following is strictly forbidden:

  • Stolen Goods: Listing or selling any item you do not have the legal right to own is illegal and will result in an immediate ban and report to authorities.
  • Counterfeit Products: Selling fake or knock-off items advertised as genuine is considered fraud.
  • Illegal Items or Services: Any items that are illegal to possess or sell are prohibited.
  • Scam Listings: Posting listings for non-existent items with the intent to collect prepayments is strictly forbidden.

Violating these rules will lead to immediate legal action .
