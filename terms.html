Terms & Conditions

Your guide to safe and fair trading in our community.
Last Updated: August 9, 2025

Welcome to Kabale Online! Our mission is to connect buyers and sellers within the Kigezi region. By using our services, you agree to follow these terms to ensure a safe, trustworthy, and fair environment for everyone.

1. Our Role & Important Disclaimer

Kabale Online is a platform that connects people. We are not a party to any transaction. We do not handle payments, ship items, guarantee transactions, or take possession of any goods.

While we will actively enforce our policies by removing users who violate our rules, **your safety and the outcome of your transactions are your own responsibility.** We are not liable for any loss, damage, or disputes that may occur between users.

2. Rules for All Users

To maintain the integrity of the marketplace, all users must adhere to the following:

  • No Stolen Goods: Listing stolen property is a criminal offense and will be reported to the authorities.
  • No Counterfeit Products: All items must be genuine. Misrepresentation is forbidden.
  • No Scam Listings: Posting non-existent items to collect prepayments or personal data is fraud.
  • No Harassment: Abusive, threatening, or hateful language towards any user will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

3. Your Responsibilities as a Seller

Honesty and clear communication are key to successful sales. As a seller, you agree to:

  • Describe Accurately: Provide truthful, detailed descriptions of your items, including any flaws, damages, or defects.
  • Use Real Photos: Post clear, recent photos of the actual item you are selling. Stock photos are not permitted.
  • Communicate Fairly: Respond to potential buyers promptly and politely. Do not pressure them into making a purchase.
  • Sell Legitimate Items: You must own and have the legal right to sell every item you list.

4. Your Responsibilities as a Buyer

Your safety is our top priority. We've created these guidelines to protect you from scams and ensure a positive experience:

  • Meet in Public & Safely: Always arrange to meet sellers in a well-lit, public place during the daytime if possible.
  • Inspect Before Paying: Thoroughly check the item to ensure it works and matches the seller's description *before* any money changes hands.
  • Pay Only Upon Exchange: Never send advance payments, especially through mobile money. Pay only when you have received the item.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If a deal feels too good to be true, a seller is being evasive, or you feel uncomfortable for any reason, it's okay to walk away.

5. Enforcement & Termination

We reserve the right to remove any listing or suspend/ban any user account at our sole discretion, without notice, for any violation of these terms or for any activity we deem harmful to the community.
