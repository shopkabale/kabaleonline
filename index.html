How to Buy on Kabale Online

  1. Browse & Discover: Explore the shop or use the search bar to find exactly what you're looking for, from textbooks to electronics.
  2. Add to Cart: Found something you like? Simply add it to your shopping cart to save it for later.
  3. Confirm Your Order: When you're ready, proceed to checkout. Your order details will be sent directly to the seller.
  4. Arrange Delivery: The seller will contact you via the phone number you provided to arrange payment and a safe, convenient meetup for delivery.

How to Sell in Minutes

  1. Create a Listing: Upload your item in under 60 seconds. Add clear photos, a good title, and an honest description.
  2. Get Notified: When a buyer places an order, you'll receive a notification. Check your seller dashboard for all the details.
  3. Contact the Buyer: Use the buyer's contact information to call or message them and arrange a time and public place for the exchange.
  4. Get Paid & Deliver: Meet the buyer, get paid (we recommend cash or mobile money), and hand over the item. Finally, mark the order as "Delivered" in your dashboard.
